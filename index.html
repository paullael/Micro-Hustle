About Us

Our Mission

At Micro Hustle, our mission is to empower individuals to find flexible, short-term gigs that fit their schedule and skills, while also helping local communities connect with reliable talent for their everyday needs.

Our Team

We are a passionate team dedicated to creating a seamless and efficient platform for micro-tasking. We believe in the power of community and the value of every hustle.

Our Story

Micro Hustle was founded in 2024 with the vision of making side gigs accessible to everyone, from students to retirees. We started small, but our community has grown rapidly, connecting thousands of hustlers with opportunities.
